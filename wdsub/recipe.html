My Famous Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Prep Work and Ingredients

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. 4 pieces of bread
  3. Shredded cheddar cheese
  4. Oven pan

Directions

  1. First you take the oven pan out from the oven
  2. Second you put the 4 pieces of bread on the oven pan
  3. Third you put the shredded cheddar cheese on the bread
  4. Then you take two of the pieces of bread and put them on top of the other two pieces of bread
  5. You then put the bread in the oven and watch the cheese melt
  6. Take out the pan and put the grilled cheese sandwich on the plate
  7. Enjoy
