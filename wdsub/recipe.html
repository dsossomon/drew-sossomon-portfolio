THE "U.S. WALL" GRILLED CHEESE

A goalkeeper's favorite pre-game fuel.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Slices of Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Slice of Provolone
  • butter on top of the bread

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread generously. Sprinkle a tiny bit of garlic powder on the butter.
  3. Place one slice butter-side down in the pan. Layer your cheeses on top.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter-side up.
  5. Grill for 3-4 minutes per side until the bread is "Energy Blue" level golden brown and the cheese is melted.
